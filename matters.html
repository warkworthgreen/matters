The aims of Warkworth Green Matters shall be:
  • To inform, influence and act to facilitate greener ways of living in and around Warkworth.
  • To design and implement practical projects, usually through WGM sub-groups, that protect nature, conserve wildlife and increase climate mitigation, adaptation and resilience in the local community.
  • To engage members of the community in this work, through information, education and participation, and generally in accordance with the nature recovery and climate action policies of Northumberland County Council.
